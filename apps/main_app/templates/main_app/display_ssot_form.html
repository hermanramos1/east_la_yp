What is the SSOT?

The Summer School of Truth (SSOT) is a week-long bible school for young people. This is a special time for the young people in the church to gather together to enjoy the Lord, to be constituted with the truth in the Word, and to be built up together in Christ. This annual summer bible school has engaging Bible classes and fun activities.

SSOT 2019 Information

  • Cost: 65.00
  • Location: Church in East Los Angeles
  • Dates: July 29th-August 3rd
  • Registration Deadline: June 30th
